About Plex Donate

Plex Donate is a community-powered media sharing platform that enables content creators and library curators to share their Plex media libraries with supporters through a subscription-based model.

Our Purpose

We provide a streamlined solution for managing Plex library access, handling subscription billing, and facilitating community growth through referral systems. Our platform helps library owners:

  • Accept recurring donations via PayPal
  • Automatically manage Plex user invitations and access
  • Track subscriber status and billing lifecycle
  • Enable community growth through referral invite systems
  • Maintain secure, password-protected account management

How It Works

Library supporters create an account, link their Plex identity, and choose a subscription plan. Once active, supporters gain access to the shared media library and can invite others to join the community. Library administrators maintain full control over access, billing settings, and user management through a dedicated dashboard.

Platform Features

  • Automated Access Management: Seamless Plex invitation handling with automatic access grants and revocations
  • PayPal Billing: Support for PayPal payment processing
  • Trial System: Optional 7-day trial periods for new supporters
  • Referral System: Community growth through monthly referral invites
  • Email Notifications: Automated verification and status updates
